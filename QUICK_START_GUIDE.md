## 🎯 **Student Quick Start** (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Explore the Dashboard
- Click **"Get Started"** on the Welcome page
- You'll see your **Dashboard** with:
  - Overall progress
  - Recent activity
  - Quick stats
  - Mastery levels for concepts

### Step 2: Choose a Concept to Learn
- Click **"Start Learning"** or go to **Learning Path**
- You'll see **5 chapters** with **8+ concepts**
- Start with **Chapter 1, Concept 1** (recommended)
- Concepts are unlocked progressively (complete prerequisites first)

### Step 3: Study the Content
- Click on any available concept (not locked)
- You'll see **4 tabs:**
  1. **📚 Theory** - Read explanations and watch videos
  2. **💡 Examples** - Story-based real-world problems
  3. **🎮 Interactive** - Games and activities
  4. **✅ Practice** - Link to assessment

### Step 4: Take the Assessment
- Click **"Start Practice Questions"** or the **Practice tab**
- Answer questions one by one
- **If you get it wrong:**
  - Wait 1 second → **Hint 1 appears automatically**
  - Try again
  - Wrong again? → **Hint 2 appears**
  - Continue through **Hint 3** and **Hint 4**
  - After all hints → **"View Complete Solution" button appears**
  
### Step 5: Track Your Progress
- After completing questions, see your:
  - ✅ Mastery percentage
  - 🎯 Points earned
  - 🏆 Badges unlocked
  - 📊 Performance analytics

## 🎮 **Progressive Hint System - How It Works**

### Example Session:

```
1. You see Question 1:
   "Five students scored 80, 85, 90, 75, 95. What is their mean?"

2. You select an answer: "90"

3. You click "Submit Answer"

4. ❌ System says: "Not quite right"
   - [WAIT 1 SECOND]
   - 💡 Hint 1 appears: "Remember the formula: Mean = Sum ÷ Count"

5. You try again with Hint 1: Select "83"

6. Still wrong! ❌
   - [WAIT 1 SECOND]
   - 💡 Hint 2 appears: "First add all marks: 80+85+90+75+95"

7. You try again: Select "85"

8. ✅ Correct! Well done!
   - 🎉 Confetti animation!
   - +20 points earned
   - Streak: 1
```

### If You Still Struggle After All 4 Hints:

```
After Hint 4, if still incorrect:
   ↓
"View Complete Solution" button appears
   ↓
Click it to see:
   ✅ Correct Answer highlighted
   ✅ Step-by-step explanation
   ✅ Common mistakes section
   ✅ Why wrong answers are wrong
   ↓
Study the solution carefully
   ↓
Click "Next Question"
```

## ❓ **Common Questions**

### Q: What if I don't understand a hint?
**A:** Keep reading it carefully. Each hint gives you more information. After Hint 4, you can view the complete solution.

### Q: Can I skip concepts?
**A:** No, concepts are unlocked progressively. You need to master prerequisite concepts first (60%+ mastery).

### Q: How do I earn badges?
**A:** Badges are earned automatically when you meet certain criteria (e.g., 3 correct in a row, completing without hints, etc.)

### Q: What does the BKT probability mean?
**A:** It's the system's estimate of how likely you are to answer questions correctly on this concept. Higher = better understanding.

### Q: Can I retake assessments?
**A:** Yes! You can practice concepts multiple times to improve mastery.

### Q: How long does it take to master a concept?
**A:** Typically 15-30 minutes per concept, depending on difficulty and your prior knowledge.
